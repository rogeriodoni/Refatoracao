**Fix applied** ÔÇö three changes in `AtualizarPeso` at lines 182ÔÇô206:

2. **Line 188 (was error)**: `ALLTRIM((loc_cCompo)->Mats)` ÔåÆ pre-captured to `loc_cMats`, used directly in SQL string
3. **Line 200 (was error)**: `IIF(..., (loc_cCompo)->Qtds, (loc_cCompo)->Pesos)` ÔåÆ pre-captured to `loc_nQtds`/`loc_nPesos`, used in IIF directly
