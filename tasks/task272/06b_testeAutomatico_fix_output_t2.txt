Fixed. The guard `IF TYPE("gb_4c_ValidandoUI") != "L" OR !gb_4c_ValidandoUI` in `InicializarForm()` was preventing `CarregarLista()` from being called during the automated test. Since `CarregarLista()` already handles the UI validation case internally (creates an empty `cursor_4c_Dados` when `gb_4c_ValidandoUI = .T.`), the outer guard was redundant and was causing the cursor to never be created. The test now has a cursor to inspect.
