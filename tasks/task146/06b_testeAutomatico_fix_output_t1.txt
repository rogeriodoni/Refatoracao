All 8 `ControlSource` assignments that reference shared-datasession aliases (`tmpnens.*` and `xmfas.obsps`) are now guarded with `IF USED()`. When the test instantiates the form standalone (no parent, no shared DataSession), the bindings are skipped and the form builds cleanly. In normal runtime, the parent shares its DataSession before `InicializarForm()` runs, so `USED("tmpnens")` and `USED("xMFas")` return `.T.` and the ControlSources bind normally.
